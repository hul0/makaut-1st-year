MAKAUT B.Tech 1st Year Syllabus

Complete Topic List for All Groups

Based on the 2018–2019 MAKAUT curriculum

Which Group Are You In?

Your 1st Year subjects depend on your group, which is determined by your engineering branch. Find your branch below to know your group.

Group A (Physics Cycle)

Starts with Physics, Workshop, etc. in Sem 1.

  • Computer Science & Engineering (CSE)
  • Information Technology (IT)
  • Mechanical Engineering, Production Engineering
  • Civil Engineering, Automobile Engineering
  • Marine Engineering, Apparel Production
  • Bio-Technology, Food Technology
  • Leather Technology, Textile Technology
  • Ceramic Technology, Chemical Engineering

Group B (Chemistry Cycle)

Starts with Chemistry, Engg. Graphics, etc. in Sem 1.

  • Electrical Engineering (EE)
  • Electronics & Communication (ECE)
  • Applied Electronics & Instrumentation (AEIE)
  • Power Engineering, Electrical & Electronics (EEE)
  • Bio-Medical Engineering (BME)
  • Instrumentation & Control (ICE)

Semester 1

Group A

Physics-I

BS-PH101 (3-1-0, 4 Cr)

Mechanics (7L): Constraints, friction, potential energy, oscillations, rigid body motion.
Optics (5L): Diffraction, polarization, Lasers.
Electromagnetism (8L): Maxwell’s equations, dielectric/magnetic properties.
Quantum Mechanics (16L): Compton effect, de Broglie hypothesis, Schrödinger equation.
Statistical Mechanics (8L): Macro/Microstate, M-B, F-D, B-E statistics.
Group B

Chemistry-I

BS-CH101 (3-1-0, 4 Cr)

Atomic & Molecular Structure (10L): Schrödinger eq, MO theory, Crystal field theory, band structure.
Spectroscopic Techniques (8L): Electronic, vibrational, rotational spectroscopy, NMR, MRI.
Thermodynamics (8L): Free energy, Nernst eq, corrosion, Ellingham diagrams.
Periodic Properties & Stereochemistry (8L): Effective nuclear charge, configurations, isomerism.
Organic Reactions (4L): Substitution, addition, elimination, oxidation, reduction.
All Groups

Mathematics–I

IA for CSE/IT, IB for others

Math-IA (BS-M101): Calculus (Integration & Differentiation), Matrices, Vector Spaces, Eigenvalues/Eigenvectors.
Math-IB (BS-M102): Calculus (Integration & Differentiation), Sequence & Series, Fourier Series, Multivariate Calculus, Matrices.
All Groups

Basic Electrical Engineering

ES-EE101 (3-1-0, 4 Cr)

DC & AC Circuits (16H): KCL/KVL, Thevenin/Norton, RL/RC circuits, phasors, RLC resonance, 3-phase circuits.
Transformers & Machines (14H): Ideal/practical transformer, induction & DC motors, synchronous generators.
Converters & Installations (12H): Buck/boost converters, inverters, LT switchgear, earthing.

Semester 1 Labs

Group A

Physics-I Lab (BS-PH191): 10 experiments on Optics, E&M, Quantum Physics etc.

Group B

Chemistry-I Lab (BS-CH191): 10 experiments like Conductometric/pH-metric titration, DO analysis etc.

All Groups

Basic Electrical Engg. Lab (ES-EE191): 10 experiments on RLC circuits, transformers, motors.

Group A

Workshop/Manufacturing (ES-ME192): Practice in Machine shop, Fitting, Carpentry, Welding etc.

Group B

Engg. Graphics & Design (ES-ME191): Projections, Sections, CAD.

Mandatory Programs

Induction Program (3 Weeks)

Pre-Semester 1

  • Week 1: Institute introduction, mentor assignment, lectures by eminent personalities, games/yoga, department visits.
  • Week 2: Proficiency modules (English/computers), creative arts, sports.
  • Week 3: Visits to neighbourhood, natural spots, science museums, NGOs.

Mandatory Additional Requirement

MAR (100 Activity Points)

Points to be earned across all semesters for degree eligibility through activities like:

  • MOOCs (SWAYAM/NPTEL), Tech Fests, Tree Plantation.
  • Debates, Quizzes, Research Publications, Innovative Projects.
  • Blood Donation, Sports, Cultural Programs, Industry Visits.
  • Community Service, Entrepreneurship, and more.
